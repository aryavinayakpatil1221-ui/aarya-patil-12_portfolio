From curiosity to code

My journey into technology didn't start with a single moment of inspiration — it grew gradually, project by project. What began as curiosity about how digital products work turned into a genuine drive to build, design, and solve real problems with code.

I'm drawn to Artificial Intelligence because it sits at the intersection of logic and creativity — the idea that a system can learn, adapt, and assist people in meaningful ways fascinates me. At the same time, I love Web Development for its immediacy: an idea can become something tangible, interactive, and usable in a browser within hours.

Alongside the technical side, I care deeply about UI/UX Design — because the best technology means nothing if people can't understand or enjoy using it. I try to bring leadership, clear communication, and structured project management to every team I work with.

Looking ahead, my goal is to grow into a well-rounded Software Engineer who can move fluidly between AI systems, web platforms, and thoughtful design — building products that are not just functional, but genuinely intelligent and human-centered.
